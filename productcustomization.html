Brand Recognition

Brand awareness is essential to your company's success, as should be obvious. We understand that you want your brand to be noticeable when deploying. So you may fully meet your branding and marketing requirements, you can change the printing on our items and/or their packaging. For example, you can promote your fibre subscriptions using a QR code, making it easier for customers to learn about the fibre subscriptions you offer. Alternately, add a barcode to the FTU or active device to allow installers to attach a home connection to a particular spot and active device.





Customized assembly

As an operator, you want the deployment to be as simple and quick as possible. For example by pre-assembling specific products or their attachments. You can minimize the costly work of installers in the field by using pre-assembled components, such as a shielded fibre cable or a FiberTwist-FTU with pre-installed pigtails. increasing the number of deployments each day and lowering the total cost of ownership (TCO). Together with you, we look for the most efficient method of product delivery for quick and simple deployment.





Default network Configuration

There is no one firmware version that can function for all possible networks out of the box without any adjustments because every deployment scenario is unique. This is addressed by homefiber using a clever and straightforward customizing method. This implies that a home fiber device immediately receives all the necessary network settings when you connect it to your network. Devices can be pre-configured by homefiber before being delivered to you. In either case, homefiber helps you make the installation process as simple as possible.





Sustainability

The concept of sustainability is getting more and more attention today. We have developed products with housings made of more than 95% recycled plastics in response to this expanding market demand. Parallel to this, we are using less paper and plastic in our products and are constantly exploring new ways to reduce our impact on the environment.
