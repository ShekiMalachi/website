RAPID TROUBLESHOOTING

CloudSight Helpdesk

Customer service tools to monitor home networks and fix service disruptions are available through CloudSight Helpdesk. Network problems are identified and displayed in easy-to-read KPI dashboards at the system level.

Alarms are issued when something goes wrong, allowing for proactive problem solving. A customer can be quickly located by the call centre representative using the flexible search options. The customer-level dashboards offer a thorough assessment of the home network's health, identify any potential weak points, and offer action buttons to address the problem.





AT YOUR SERVICE EVERY DAY

CloudSight Assistance

CloudSight Support offers first-rate service around-the-clock. You can rely on expert guidance on how CloudSight will function most effectively in your particular circumstance. Our group makes sure that the installation goes well and supports you completely with the configuration, integration, expert and non-expert training, and onboarding. Post-installation quality is guaranteed by proactive measuring performance and quick assurance help.





CONVENIENT SYSTEM INTEGRATION

Cloudsight API

A third-party application or your OSS/BSS environment can be quickly connected to CloudSight via the CloudSight API. CloudSight may be incorporated using REST-API into your existing customer service and billing system, issue ticketing, network inventory, business analytics, and performance management platforms. End-to-end service delivery can be automated and made possible Your order management system can be linked to CloudSight.





SAFE CLOUD ACCESS WITH CLOUDSIGHT HOSTING

CloudSight Hosting

CloudSight can be deployed in a number of different ways. You have the choice to link CloudSight to a local server as an operator or ISP. As an alternative, consider using CloudSight Hosting services. a managed service that provides CloudSight with a stable, secure, and scalable server environment. A group of professionals provides certification, configuration, and security upgrades while also keeping track of and reporting on performance. You can count on a high-quality, managed server environment with CloudSight Hosting. All servers at CloudSight Hosting are situated in the EU and adhere to GDPR regulations.
