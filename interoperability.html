Compatibility with the leading OLT provider

In actuality, this means that a network operator having many OLT suppliers in its networks does not have to worry about which OLT is on the other end of the ONT. Because the single firmware image recognises which OLT is on the other side and adjusts accordingly, the many OLTs can use Infotech ONTs, such as the FiberTwist.





Install applications for external users

Together with preventing vendor lock-in on the PON side, we also guarantee interoperability with third parties in other areas. Our products communicate with other companies' products without any issues due to our software, which is built on these open standards. In other words, the Infotech product will always connect and work without fail.





No vendor lock-in

The last thing you want when constructing a PON network with a specific OLT or when employing a specific home gateway is to be totally locked in with your future choices. Infotech, an independent provider, acts exactly the opposite. We design products that are completely compatible to prevent vendor lock-in. As part of our open philosophy, we constantly support the greatest number of OLTs and independent residential gateways. In other words, your decision today has no bearing on your decision afterward.





Certified for XGS-PON

With a focus on ONT configuration (OMCI) and QoS, the FiberTwist XGS2110x has been certified for the Optical Network Termination (ONT) compliance test plan. It strives to guarantee ONTs' development and adherence to the market's and operators' requirements. It creates the conditions for integration and compatibility
